Design a modern web platform for educators to connect, collaborate, share resources, and grow professionally.

The platform should function as a hybrid of a professional network, community forum, resource hub, and lesson planning tool specifically designed for educators.

The platform should feel like a modern social platform optimized for teachers, prioritizing ease of use, quick navigation, and meaningful professional collaboration.

The design must include:

a design system
reusable components
UX flows
high-fidelity UI screens
responsive layouts
Product Concept

Create a platform where educators can:

connect with other educators
share teaching resources
build and share lesson plans
discuss classroom ideas and challenges
discover inspiring education content
find job opportunities in education

The audience includes:

teachers
school administrators
parents interested in education
education enthusiasts

The product should feel professional, supportive, and collaborative.

Teachers are busy professionals, so the platform must be intuitive and efficient.

Visual Design

Style should be:

Modern
Clean
Professional
Social-media inspired
Minimal and easy to navigate

Avoid overly playful or childish design.

Color System

Primary color: Dark Maroon

Secondary color: Grey

Background: Light neutral or soft grey

Accent color: subtle off-white or light accent for highlights

Typography should be clean, readable, and professional.
